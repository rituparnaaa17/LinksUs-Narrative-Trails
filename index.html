Who We Are

Narrative Trails is a Mumbai-based educational travel outfit led by Pratap Patil (+91 82084 83065). We craft treks and immersive walks around Maharashtra’s natural and cultural heritage.

Our facilitators bring stories alive—linking geography with history, biodiversity with livelihoods. We prioritise safety, context, and joyful learning outdoors.

  • End-to-end planning: routes, permissions, logistics & safety
  • Age-appropriate learning goals for schools & colleges
  • Corporate day-outs with nature, teamwork & local food
